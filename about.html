About

Citizens of the Internet is a personal website gallery that features unique, thoughtful and expertly-crafted personal websites from across the internet.

Approximately 5.52 billion people on Planet Earth, or 69% of the global population, use the internet every day. Of these users, perhaps only 1% or less have their own personal website.

This gallery is dedicated to that 1%, to the nerd necks who have staked their claim of the information superhighway and built their own hypertext home.

Citizens is created and curated by Ben Hatch.
